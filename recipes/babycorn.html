Baby Corn

Description

Baby corn (also known as young corn or baby sweetcorn) is a grain taken from corn. It is harvested early stage while the corn stalks is still small and immature. These are usually used in making salads, appetizers and other recipes.

Baby corn can be consumed whole when it is still young. If the corn matures the inner core gets tough which makes it inedible.

Ingredients

  • Spice powders
  • Flour Mixture
  • Ginger & Garlic
  • Lemon Juice
  • Oil

Steps :

  1. 1.

    Take baby corn and peel off the outer husk and wash it really well. Nowadays peeled baby corn is also available in the market.

  2. 2.

    Cut baby corn into small pieces. You can slit them in the middle lengthwise. That makes it look like a french fry.

  3. 3.

    Take baby corn in a sauce pan or kadai.

  4. 4.

    Cover the baby corn with water.

  5. 5.

    Add in salt and mix well.
